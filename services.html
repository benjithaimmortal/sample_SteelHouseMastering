Mastering
Commercial level loudness, clarity, power, and punch. All tracks delivered in your choice of format complete with digital m3u file, and ready for online and physical media distribution.
Stem Mastering
Stem mastering is in between mixing and mastering. If you're not confident in your final mix, stem mastering may be your ticket. Send us the groups and busses (drums, guitars, keys, bass, vocals, ect.) Stem Mastering gives more control to your final representation and mix.
Mixing
Ah, mixing. The culmination of all your hard work. Your track needs to move and breathe. It needs depth, and everything needs to be heard clearly. Let us get you there!
Additional services are available, such as drum replacement, tuning, and quantizing.
Price is dependent on amount of tracks, and editing involved. Message for more info.
Production, Recording, Sessions
Whether it's playing an instrument on a track of yours, writing, or recording, let us know what you're looking for. We collaborate with studios, and we maintain a list of instrumentalists and writers across the country. Price greatly varies from project to project, so give us an idea of what you're looking for and we can give you a quote.
